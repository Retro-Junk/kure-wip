Game versions differences

Amiga EU:
16-color graphics with palette effects
Digitized sounds
Intro music track
English, German and French languages

Amiga US:
16-color graphics with palette effects
Digitized sounds
Intro music track
English language only

Atari ST EU:
16-color graphics with palette effects
Digitized sounds
English, German and French languages

Atari ST US:
Wanted!

PC EU
4-color CGA or monochrome Hercules graphics (on Hercules doubled horizontal resolution is used for dithering effect)
PC-speaker sounds (beeps and chirps)
English, German and French languages (plus unselectable Spanish language?)

PC US
4-color CGA or monochrome Hercules graphics (on Hercules doubled horizontal resolution is used for dithering effect)
16-color EGA or Tandy graphics (no palette effects), some visual effects removed
PC-speaker sounds
Adlib FM sounds and ambients
English language only
